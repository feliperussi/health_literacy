Overall, 407 patients received at least one dose of the study medicine. The study
included 264 (65%) men and 143 (35%) women. The average age was 49 years. The
youngest patient was 18 years old and the oldest patient was 82 years old.
For more detailed information about the patients included in this study, see the
scientific results summaries (links to those summaries are provided at the end of this
document).

What were the main results of the study?
The main results of the study included assessments of nasal polyp size, nasal
obstruction score, and the risk of having nasal polyp surgery.
