How has this study helped patients and researchers?
This study helped researchers better understand how FFNS works in Chinese children
with allergic rhinitis.
Are there plans for further studies?
There are no plans for further studies of FFNS in children with allergic rhinitis. One
study of FFNS in Chinese adult patients with allergic rhinitis is ongoing.
