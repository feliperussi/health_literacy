What were the side effects?
Unwanted medical problems (adverse events) can happen to people when they receive a
study medicine. Study doctors record these events.
In this summary, side effects refer to those events that the study doctor thinks may have
been caused by the study medicine. The side effects in this summary may be different to
those in the Informed Consent or other documents related to the study medicine.
A summary of all events reported in this study may be found in the clinical results summary.
What were the serious side effects?
Serious side effects were reported by nine participants (22%). The only serious side effect
reported by two or more participants was decrease in a type of blood cells that help stop
bleeding, in three participants (7%). No fatal serious side effects were reported by
participants in the study.
