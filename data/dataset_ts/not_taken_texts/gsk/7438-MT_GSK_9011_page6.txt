The detailed title for this research study is:
A Phase 2, randomised, observer-blind, multi-centre study to evaluate the safety, reactogenicity and
immunogenicity of GSK Biologicals' GSK3277511A investigational vaccine when administered
intramuscularly according to two different vaccine schedules in adults aged 40 to 80 years old.
