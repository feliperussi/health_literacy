4. Who Was Included in This Study?
Who took part in the study?
This study included 251 participants with moderate-to-severe AD. 109 participants
(43%, or about 43 out of 100) were women and 142 participants (57%, or about 57
out of 100) were men. They ranged in age from 18 years old to 71 years old.
125 participants (50%, or 50 out of 100) were age 35 years old or younger and 50%
were older than 36 years old.
This study took place at 78 study centers across Australia, Canada, Czech Republic,
Estonia, Germany, Hungary, Japan, Latvia, Poland, Republic of Korea, Spain,
Ukraine, the United Kingdom, and the United States.
