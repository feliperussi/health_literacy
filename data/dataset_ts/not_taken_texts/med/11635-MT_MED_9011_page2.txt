Who took part in this study?
The researchers asked for the help of healthy men and women. The participants
in this study were 18 to 50 years old when they joined.
The participants could join the study if they:
X had never had a medical condition that could make it unsafe to take part in
this study.
X had never had a medical condition that could change how the study
treatments worked in the body.
The study included 62 participants in Germany.
