What were the results of the study?
This is a summary of the main results from this study overall. The results each
participant had might be different and are not in this summary. A full list of the
questions researchers wanted to answer can be found on the websites listed at
the end of this summary. If a full report of the study results is available, it can also
be found on these websites.
Researchers look at the results of many studies to decide which treatments
work best and are safest. Other studies may provide new information or different
results. Always talk to a doctor before making any treatment changes.
There were 11 participants who did not finish taking all of their study treatments.
So, the results below include information for 1,066 out of 1,077 participants.
