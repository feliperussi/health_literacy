Clinical Study Results
What adverse reactions did the participants have?
The most common adverse reaction was vomiting.
The table below shows the adverse reactions that happened in at least 5 participants
during the study. There were other adverse reactions, but these happened in fewer
participants.
Most common adverse reactions
After taking the first
MEDI0382 dose
(out of 22 participants)
Vomiting 77.3% (17)
Nausea 63.6% (14)
Headache 45.5% (10)
Decreased appetite 36.4% (8)
Dizziness 22.7% (5)
Stomach pain 22.7% (5)
How has this study helped patients and researchers?
This study helped researchers learn how MEDI0382 affects the blood and the heart rate
in people who are taking blood thinners and heart medicine.
Researchers look at the results of many studies to decide which treatments work best
and are safest. This summary shows only the main results from this one study. Other
studies may provide new information or different results.
Further clinical studies with MEDI0382 are planned.
