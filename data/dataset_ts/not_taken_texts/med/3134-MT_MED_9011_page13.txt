What serious adverse reactions happened during this study?
There were 1.3% of participants who got the placebo who had a serious adverse
reaction. This was 1 out of 76 participants. The serious adverse reaction was a
headache. None of the participants who got tezepelumab had a serious adverse
reaction.
None of the participants died due to a serious adverse reaction.
