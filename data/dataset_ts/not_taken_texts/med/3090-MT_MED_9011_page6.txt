How many participants had serious adverse reactions?
None of the participants had serious adverse reactions during the study.
None of the participants died during the study.
How many participants had adverse reactions?
There were 50.8% of participants who had adverse reactions during the study. This was
33 out of 65 participants.
There were 1.5% of participants who stopped taking MEDI0382 because of an adverse
reaction they had during the study. This was 1 out of 65 participants.
