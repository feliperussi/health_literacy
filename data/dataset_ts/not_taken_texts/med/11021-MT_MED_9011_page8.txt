Clinical Study Results
How long it took for budesonide to reach its highest amount in the
blood
Overall, the researchers found that:
• It took 0.36 hours for budesonide to reach its highest amount in the blood
when the participants took budesonide combined with albuterol sulfate.
• It took 0.33 hours for budesonide to reach its highest amount in the blood
when the participants took Pulmicort, which contained only budesonide.
