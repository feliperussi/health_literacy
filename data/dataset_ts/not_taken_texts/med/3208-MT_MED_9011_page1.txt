Clinical Study Results
Research Sponsor: AstraZeneca Gothenburg
Drugs Studied: Budesonide, glycopyrronium and formoterol fumarate,
also called BGF
Study Title: A study to learn how much BGF got into the lungs of
healthy participants.
Thank you!
Thank you to the participants who took part in the clinical trial for the study
drugs budesonide, glycopyrronium and formoterol fumarate, also called BGF.
AstraZeneca Gothenburg sponsored this study and thinks it is important to share
the results. An independent non-profit organization called CISCRP helped prepare
this summary of the study results for you.
If you participated in the study and have questions about the results, please
speak with the doctor or staff at your study site.
What is happening with the study now?
The study started in September 2018 and ended in October 2018. The sponsor
reviewed the data collected when the study ended and created a report of the
results. This is a summary of that report.
The study included 10 participants from the United Kingdom.
