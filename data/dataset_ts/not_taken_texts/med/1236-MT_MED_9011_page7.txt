Clinical Study Results
How has this study helped patients and
researchers?
These results helped the researchers learn more about using benralizumab in
an auto-injection device to help people with asthma.
Researchers look at the results of many studies to decide which treatments
work best and are safest. This summary shows only the main results from this
one study. Other studies may provide new information or different results.
Further clinical studies of the auto-injection device are not planned at the time
this summary was written.
