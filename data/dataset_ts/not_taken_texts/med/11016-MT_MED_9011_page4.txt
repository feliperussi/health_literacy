Who took part in this study?
The researchers asked for the help of men and women with a type of heart failure
called HFpEF. The participants in this study were 41 to 92 years old when they
joined. People with other types of heart failure did not participate in this study.
The study included 504 participants in 12 countries. These were Argentina, Brazil,
Bulgaria, Canada, Denmark, Italy, Japan, Slovakia, South Africa, South Korea,
Sweden, and the United States.
