What were the results of the study?
This is a summary of the main results from this study overall. The results each
participant had might be different and are not in this summary. A full list of the
questions researchers wanted to answer can be found on the websites listed
at the end of this summary. If a full report of the study results is available, it can
also be found on these websites.
Researchers look at the results of many studies to decide which treatments
work best and are safest. Other studies may provide new information or different
results. Always talk to a doctor before making any treatment change.
How did dapagliflozin affect the fat levels in the body compared
to glimepiride?
Overall, the researchers found that the participants who took dapagliflozin
and metformin had a larger decrease in their body fat levels compared to
the participants who took glimepiride and metformin. Participants who took
glimepiride and metformin had increased body fat levels.
