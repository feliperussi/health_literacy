What adverse reactions happened during this study?
There were 87.1% of participants who had medical problems that the study
doctors thought might be related to the study drug during the study. This was 243
out of 279 participants. The most common adverse reaction was nausea.
The table below shows the adverse reactions that happened in 5.0% or more
of participants during the study. There were other adverse reactions, but these
happened in fewer participants.
