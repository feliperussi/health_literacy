Clinical Study Results
There were 4 different treatment groups in the study. The chart below shows the different
treatments the participants took.
Standard dose of BGF MDI 2,157 participants
2 puffs a day through an MDI inhaler
Low dose of BGF MDI 2,137 participants
2 puffs a day through an MDI inhaler
GFF MDI 2,143 participants
2 puffs a day through an MDI inhaler
BFF MDI 2,151 participants
2 puffs a day through an MDI inhaler
What happened during the study?
The participants were in the study for up to 14 months. But, the entire study took 4 years to finish.
The study started in July 2015 and ended in July 2019.
