Who took part in this study?
The researchers asked for the help of Japanese males and females with severe
asthma that was not well controlled. The participants in this study were 15 to 76
years old when they joined. Participants could join the study if they:
X had been using a steroid inhaler for 12 months or more, and at least 1 other
treatment to control their asthma for 3 months or more
X had at least 1 severe asthma attack in the last year that caused them to take
additional medication or go to the hospital
X had completed a survey that showed that their asthma was not well controlled
The study included 65 participants in Japan.
