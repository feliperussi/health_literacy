Who took part in this study?
The researchers asked for the help of men and women with obesity and either:
X non-alcoholic fatty liver disease, also called NAFLD
X non-alcoholic steatohepatitis, also called NASH
The participants in this study were 21 to 79 years old when they joined. The
participants in this study had:
X a body mass index, also called BMI, of more than 30 kilograms per meter
squared.
X evidence of NAFLD or NASH from a magnetic resonance imaging scan, also
called an MRI scan.
If the participants had diabetes, they had to meet certain criteria for how well their
diabetes was controlled, and what medications they were taking.
The study included 74 participants in the United States and Puerto Rico.
