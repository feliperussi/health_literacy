Clinical Study Results
Research Sponsor: AstraZeneca
Drug Studied: AZD9977
Study Purpose: This study was done to learn how AZD9977
worked compared to spironolactone in
participants with heart failure who have
mild or moderate kidney disease
Protocol Number: D6401C00004
Thank you!
Thank you for taking part in the clinical study for the study drug AZD9977.
AstraZeneca AB sponsored this study and believes it is important to share the results. An
independent non-profit organization called CISCRP helped prepare this summary of the
study results for you.
If you participated in the study and have questions about the results, please speak with
the study doctor or staff at your study site.
