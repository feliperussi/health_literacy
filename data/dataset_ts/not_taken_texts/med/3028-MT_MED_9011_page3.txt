What medical problems did the participants have during
this study?
There were 89.8% of participants who had medical problems that the study doctors
thought might be related to the study drugs during the study. The most common
medical problem was having very low levels of a type of white blood cell called a
neutrophil. This problem was seen in participants from all 3 treatment groups. More
details about the results of this study are included later in this summary.
