Who took part in this trial?
The researchers asked for the help of people with chronic kidney disease who
had low levels of iron in their blood. Having low levels of iron in the blood is also
called “anemia”, and chronic kidney disease is also called “CKD”.
The participants in this trial were 25 to 78 years old when they joined.
This trial included 9 males and 16 females in China.
