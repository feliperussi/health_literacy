Clinical Study Results
The results of these measurements are listed below.
Amount of budesonide in the blood during treatment
Overall, the researchers found that:
• The amount of budesonide in the blood when the participants took budesonide
combined with albuterol sulfate was 969 pg*h/mL.
• The amount of budesonide in the blood when the participants took Pulmicort,
which contained only budesonide, was 1,279 pg*h/mL.
