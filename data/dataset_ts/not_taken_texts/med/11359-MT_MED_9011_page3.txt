Who took part in this study?
The researchers asked for the help of men and women with COVID-19 who
needed hospital care. Participants in this study either were unable to breathe
enough oxygen on their own, or were getting extra oxygen in the hospital. The
participants in this study were 26 to 98 years old when they joined.
The study included 177 participants in Argentina, Brazil, Chile, France, India, Italy,
Japan, Mexico, Peru, Russia, South Africa, and Turkey.
