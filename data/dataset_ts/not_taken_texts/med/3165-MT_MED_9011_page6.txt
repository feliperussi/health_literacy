Clinical Study Results
How has this study helped patients and researchers?
This study helped researchers learn more about using ticagrelor in infants and
toddlers who have SCD.
Researchers look at the results of many studies to decide which treatments work
best and are safest. This summary shows only the main results from this one study.
Other studies may provide new information or different results.
Further clinical studies with ticagrelor in children who have SCD are planned.
Researchers will study the results from this and other ticagrelor studies to learn
more about this drug.
