What serious adverse reactions happened during this study?
The most common serious adverse reaction was herpes zoster, also called
shingles, which is an infection caused by the same virus as chickenpox and leads
to a painful, blistering rash in 1 part of the body. The table below shows the
serious adverse reactions that happened during the study.
