Who took part in this study?
The researchers asked for the help of men and women with severe asthma. The
participants in this study were 19 to 84 years old when they joined.
All of the participants in this study had a high level of white blood cells called
eosinophils and were already taking a type of treatment called oral corticosteroids
to control their asthma.
The study included 598 participants in Argentina, Belgium, Brazil, Canada,
Colombia, Denmark, France, Germany, Italy, Mexico, Poland, Russia, Spain,
Sweden, Taiwan, the United Kingdom, and the United States.
