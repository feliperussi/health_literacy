Clinical Study Results
Amount of budesonide in the blood during the 24 hours after treatment
Overall, the researchers found that:
• The amount of budesonide in the blood when the participants took budesonide
combined with albuterol sulfate was 916 pg*h/mL.
• The amount of budesonide in the blood when the participants took Pulmicort,
which contained only budesonide, was 1,235 pg*h/mL.
