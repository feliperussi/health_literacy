Who took part in this study?
The researchers asked for the help of people with a type of breast cancer called
“metastatic triple negative breast cancer”. The participants in this study were
women who were 29 to 83 years old when they joined.
The study included 273 participants in 15 countries: Belgium, Canada, the Czech
Republic, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, South Korea, Netherlands, Poland,
Portugal, Spain, Taiwan, the United States, and the United Kingdom.
