Study names
Short Title: A study to compare a two-medicine regimen with a three-medicine regimen
in patients with HIV-1 infection.
Full Scientific Title: A phase III, randomised, double-blind, multicentre, parallel group,
non-inferiority study evaluating the efficacy, safety, and tolerability of dolutegravir plus
lamivudine compared to dolutegravir plus tenofovir/emtricitabine in HIV-1-infected
treatment-naive adults.
ViiV Healthcare Study Number: 205543
