What happened during this study?

The participants were in the study for up to 6 months. But, the entire study took just over 19 months to finish. The study started in September 2019 and ended in May 2021.

Before the participants took study treatment:
- 1 visit
- The study doctors checked the health of the participants, took blood and urine samples to make sure they could join the study, took pictures of the participants' livers, did a physical exam, and asked about using MRI scans, the participants' medications, and any medical problems.
- The participants checked the participants' heart health and answered questionnaires about their symptoms using an electrocardiogram, also called an ECG.

While the participants were taking study treatment:
- 12 visits
- The study doctors did physical exams and asked about the participants' medications and any medical problems. They also took pictures of the participants' livers using MRI scans.
- The participants checked the participants' heart health using an electrocardiogram, also called an ECG. They took their study treatments and took blood and urine samples.

After the participants took study treatment:
- 1 visit
- The study doctors did a physical exam and asked about the participants' medications and any medical problems. They also took blood and urine samples.
- The participants checked the participants' heart health, answered questionnaires about their symptoms using an electrocardiogram, also called an ECG.

As long as needed.
