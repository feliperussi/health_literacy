What happened during this study?
The participants were in the study for approximately 30 weeks. But the entire study took around 9 months to finish. The study started in October 2020 and ended in July 2021.
Before the participants got study treatment:
- 2 visits
- The study doctors:
  - checked the health of the participants
  - checked the participants’ heart health using an electrocardiogram, also called an ECG
  - did physical exams and asked about the participants’ medications and any medical problems
  - took blood and urine samples
- up to 6 weeks
While the participants got study treatment:
- 7 visits
- The study doctors:
  - asked about the participants’ medications and any medical problems
  - checked for any reactions around the area of the body where the injection was given
  - took blood and urine samples
- The participants:
  - checked the participants’ heart health using an ECG
  - got AZD8233 or placebo injections on Days 1, 8, 29, and 57
- 12 weeks
After the participants got study treatment:
- up to 7 visits
- The study doctors:
  - asked about the participants’ medications and any medical problems
  - checked the participants’ heart health using an ECG
  - took blood and urine samples
  - checked for any reactions around the area of the body where the injection was given
- 12 weeks
