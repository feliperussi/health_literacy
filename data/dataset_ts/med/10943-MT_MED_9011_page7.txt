The chart below shows what happened during the study. 

Before the participants took study treatment:

1 visit
- The study doctors:
  - Checked the health of the participants to make sure they could join the study.
  - Did a physical exam and asked about the participants' medications and any medical problems.
  - Took blood and urine samples.
  - Checked the participants' heart health using an electrocardiogram and by doing a special type of heart ultrasound exam.
- The participants:
  - Answered questionnaires about their symptoms and quality of life.

4 weeks

While the participants took study treatment:

6 visits
- The study doctors:
  - Did a physical exam and asked about the participants' medications and any medical problems.
  - Checked the participants' heart health using an electrocardiogram and by doing a special type of heart ultrasound exam at some visits.
  - Took blood and urine samples.
- The participants:
  - Took their doses of AZD4831 or the placebo as tablets by mouth once a day at home.
  - Answered questionnaires about their symptoms.

Up to 3 months

After the participants took study treatment:

1 visit
- The study doctors:
  - Asked about the participants' medications and any medical problems.
  - Took blood and urine samples.
  - Checked the participants' heart health using an electrocardiogram and by doing a special type of heart ultrasound exam at some visits.
