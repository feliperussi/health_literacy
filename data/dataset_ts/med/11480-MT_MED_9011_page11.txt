How has this study helped patients and researchers?

This study helped researchers learn more about whether taking AZD9567 affected how much glucose got into the participants' blood compared with taking prednisolone. Researchers look at the results of many studies to decide which treatments work best and are safest. This summary shows only the main results from this one study. Other studies may provide new information or different results. Further clinical studies with AZD9567 and prednisolone are not planned.
