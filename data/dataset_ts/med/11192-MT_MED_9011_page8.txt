The chart below shows what happened during the study. Before the participants took study treatment:
- 1 visit
The study doctors checked the participants' heart health, checked the health of the participants using an electrocardiogram, also called ECG, to make sure they could join the study. They did a physical exam and asked about the participants’ medications and any medical problems. They also checked the participants’ lung health and measured the amount of air they could breathe out. They took blood and urine samples and checked how well the participants were able to do their usual daily activities.
