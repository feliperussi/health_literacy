How has this study helped patients and researchers?

This study helped researchers learn if taking fluvoxamine and/or smoking affected how much AZD4635 got into the blood of healthy participants. Researchers look at the results of many studies to decide which treatments work best and are safest. This summary shows only the main results from this one study. Other studies may provide new information or different results.

Further clinical studies with AZD4635 are planned.
