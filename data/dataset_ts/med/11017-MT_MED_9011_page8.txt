What happened during this study?
Each of the participants was in the study for up to just over 20 weeks. But, the entire study took 11 months to finish.
The study started in April 2019 and ended in March 2020.
Before the participants took study treatment up to 3 visits
The study doctors: 
- checked the health of the participants 
- answered questionnaires about their symptoms
- did a physical exam and asked about the participants’ medications and any medical problems
- took blood and urine samples
Some participants:
- checked the participants’ health heart using an electrocardiogram, also called an ECG
This part of the study took 2 weeks
While the participants took study treatment 2 visits and 1 phone call
The study doctors: 
- did a physical exam and asked about the participants’ medications and any medical problems
- took blood and urine samples
- answered questionnaires about their symptoms
- did the 6-minute walk distance test, also called 6MWD
Some participants:
- wore a device that measured their activity levels
This part of the study took 16 weeks
