What were the most common medical problems in the study?

The table below shows the most common medical problems that happened within 7 days of getting the vaccine or placebo. In both the 3 strain vaccine group and the placebo group in this study, nasal congestion was the most common medical problem.

- Strep throat 1 (0.4%) 0 (0.0%)
