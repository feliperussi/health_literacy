Clinical Study Results
Highest level of tezepelumab in the blood
Overall, the researchers found that the highest level of tezepelumab in the blood was:
- 24.4 μg/mL for the participants who got tezepelumab through the vial and syringe. This was for 103 out of 103 participants.
- 25.3 μg/mL for the participants who got tezepelumab through the accessorized pre-filled syringe. This was for 105 out of 105 participants.
- 25.8 μg/mL for the participants who got tezepelumab through the auto-injector. This was for 105 out of 107 participants.
