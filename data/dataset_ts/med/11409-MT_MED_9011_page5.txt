Clinical Study Results

About 2 weeks after the participants finished taking study treatment, the study doctors called them to ask about their asthma and any medications they were taking.

Before the participants took study treatment, the study doctors checked the participants' health and asthma symptoms. 

During the participants' 3 visits, the study doctors checked their health and asthma symptoms. 

After the participants took study treatment, the study doctors asked about the participants' health and asthma symptoms in 1 phone call. 

At home, the participants took their randomly chosen study treatment.

What were the results of the study?

This is a summary of the main results from this study overall. The results each participant had might be different and are not in this summary. A full list of the questions researchers wanted to answer can be found on the websites listed at the end of this summary. Researchers look at the results of many studies to decide which treatments work best and are safest. Other studies may provide new information or different results. Always talk to a doctor before making any treatment changes.
