What happened during this trial? The participants were in the trial for about 9 weeks. But, the entire trial took 7 months to finish. The trial started in February 2021 and ended in October 2021. The chart below shows what happened during the trial.

Before the participants received trial treatment:
- 1 visit
- The trial doctors checked the health of the participants and checked the participants' heart health using an electrocardiogram, also called an ECG.
- They did a physical exam and asked about the participants' medications and any medical problems.
- They took blood and urine samples.
- They checked to make sure the participants did not have COVID-19.

Up to 3 weeks while the participants received trial treatment:
- 3 visits
- The trial doctors did a physical exam and asked about the participants' medications and any medical problems.
- The participants received their trial treatment.
- They received a dose of liquid iron at the start and at the end of receiving trial treatment.
- They took blood and urine samples.

After the participants received trial treatment:
- 1 visit
- The trial doctors asked about the participants' medications and any medical problems.
