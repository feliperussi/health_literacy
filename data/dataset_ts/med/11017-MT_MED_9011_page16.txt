You can find more information about this study on the websites listed below. When a full report
of the study results is available, it also can be found here.

Clinical study participants and their families belong to a large community of people who take
part in clinical research around the world. They help researchers answer important health
questions and find medical treatments for patients.
The Center for Information & Study on Clinical Research Participation (CISCRP) is a non-profit organization focused on educating and informing the public about clinical research participation. CISCRP is not involved in recruiting participants for clinical studies, nor is it involved in conducting clinical studies.
