Who took part in this study?
The researchers asked for the help of men and women with a type of heart failure called HFrEF. The participants in this study were 30 to 93 years old when they joined. People with other types of heart failure did not participate in this study. The study included 313 participants in 9 countries. These were Brazil, Canada, Denmark, Japan, Slovakia, South Africa, South Korea, Sweden and the United States.
