What happened during this study?
The participants were in the study for up to 1 year and 3 months. But, the entire study took 22 months to finish. The study started in June 2019 and ended in March 2021.

Before the participants got study treatment:
- 1 visit
- The study doctors checked the health of the participants to check the participants' heart health, make sure they could join the study using an electrocardiogram (ECG), did a physical exam, and asked about the participants' lung health and medications.
- They also measured the amount of air the participants could breathe out and took blood and urine samples to check how well the participants were able to do their usual daily activities and how well their asthma was controlled.

While the participants got study treatment:
- 13 visits
- The study doctors did a physical exam and asked about the participants' medications and any medical problems.
- They checked the participants' lung health and measured the amount of air they could breathe out.
- At some visits, they also checked the participants' heart health using an ECG.

After the participants got study treatment:
- At least 2 visits
- The study doctors did a physical exam and asked about the participants' medications and any medical problems.
- They took blood and urine samples to check the participants' lung health and measured the amount of air they could breathe out.
- At some visits, they also checked the participants' heart health using an ECG.
