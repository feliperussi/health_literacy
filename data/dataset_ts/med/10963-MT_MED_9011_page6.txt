Clinical Study Results

Adverse reactions may or may not be caused by the study drug. A lot of research is needed to know whether a drug causes an adverse reaction.

The adverse reactions listed in this section are also included in the list of adverse events above.

The websites listed at the end of this summary may have more information about the adverse reactions that happened during this study.

How many participants had serious adverse reactions?
None of the participants had serious adverse reactions during the study.

How many participants had adverse reactions?
There were 5.3% of participants who had adverse reactions after taking AZD9977 during the study. This was 1 out of 19 participants.

There were 22.2% of participants who had adverse reactions after taking the placebo during the study. This was 2 out of 9 participants.
