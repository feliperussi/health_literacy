Clinical Study Results
The chart below shows how the study was done.
Before the participants took the study treatment:
- The doctors checked the health of the participants and took blood and urine samples.
During the study:
- The participants visited their study site 2 times and the doctors checked their overall health and took blood samples.
- The participants answered a questionnaire at each visit.
At home:
- The participants took either dapagliflozin or placebo once a day.
The study duration was about 17 months.
