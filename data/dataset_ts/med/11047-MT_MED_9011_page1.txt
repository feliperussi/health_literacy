Study Title: A study to learn how AZD7594 acts in the blood of adolescents with asthma

Thank you!
Thank you to the participants who took part in the clinical study for the study drug AZD7594. AstraZeneca AB sponsored this study and thinks it is important to share the results. An independent non-profit organization called CISCRP helped prepare this summary of the study results.
If you or your child participated in the study and have questions about the results, please speak with the study doctor or staff at your study site.
