Clinical Study Results

Albuterol sulfate:
- The amount of albuterol sulfate in the blood when the participants took albuterol sulfate only was 3,637 pg*h/mL. These results include 90 of the 91 participants.
- The amount of albuterol sulfate in the blood when the participants took budesonide and albuterol sulfate together was 3,373 pg*h/mL. These results include 89 of the 91 participants.

Amount of albuterol sulfate in the blood until the amounts became too low for the researchers to measure:
- Albuterol sulfate only: 3,637 pg*h/mL
- Budesonide and albuterol sulfate together: 3,373 pg*h/mL

Highest amount of the drugs in the blood during the 24 hours after treatment:
- Albuterol sulfate only: 3,637 pg*h/mL
- Budesonide and albuterol sulfate together: 3,373 pg*h/mL

Budesonide:
- The highest amount of budesonide in the blood when the participants took budesonide only was 317 pg/mL. These results include 89 of the 91 participants.
- The highest amount of budesonide in the blood when the participants took budesonide and albuterol sulfate together was 273 pg/mL. These results include 88 of the 91 participants.
